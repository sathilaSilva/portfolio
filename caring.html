CARING COMMUNITY OF SRI LANKA

UI/UX CASE STUDY FOR A COMMUNITY SERVICE GROUP:

DESCRIPTION

This is a project I designed for a web design course on Udemy.

Caring Community of Sri Lanka is a non-profit organization, which does Community Services for People who are in need. This group has done various projects over the past three years. In order to expand our group and have more charity, we decided to build a website. The primary goal of this website design is to get donations for the upcoming projects and showcase the projects we did in the past. In the meantime, we are in the process of developing it.
